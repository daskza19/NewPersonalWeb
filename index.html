ABOUT ME

Hello, I am Josep Sànchez Arbona, I'm 21 years old and I like photography, cinema and especially the conceptual art process for video games. During my life I have encountered this question:

Can different skills be used in the same task?

Taking graphic art as a focus, I have always tried to learn new things so as not to focus on just one skill. I have learned art in a self-taught way and studied the technological baccalaureate, making me also have a technical profile. I am a person who loves to learn new things and to be able to apply the things that I know in new areas.

Today I am studying the Design and Development of Videogames degree at CITM, Terrassa. A degree that mixes the two fields that I dominate the most: computer science and art. Also in February 2021 I started working at Innovamat, where I am in the QA department.

The Mandalorian: Ashes of the empire is fast-paced roguelite with an emphasis on ranged combat and constant repositioning. The player controls Din Djarin, the Mandalorian. Readying himself before a crucial mission, Mando shares a drink with his allies in a canteen, remembering and retelling a past mission in which he took on a crusade against Moff Gideon.

This video game was created on an own engine. The development lasted 3 and a half months and a team of 30 people worked on it. Within this project I was the main person in charge of the concept art of the characters and the UI.

Between Worlds is a demo video game in which you can control a protagonist capable of traveling between dimensional portals. These portals make you take advantage of the terrain to use different mechanics: climb lianas, pass between rings of fire, etc. There are intentions to remake the game with Unity and add more levels and mechanics.

This game was made by 2 people within a period of three months for the Videogame Development subject of the Videogame Design and Development degree. Because the project was organized with small groups, I did a variety of tasks. I was the base artist of the character, the main programmer (C++) and the person in charge of the final trailer of the game.

Mythology Parade is a game made by 8 people in a period of three months for the Project 2 subject of the Video Game Design and Development degree. In this project I was the art director and responsible for the creation of the trailer. For the art part I made all the UI and characters sprites and the optimization of these images.

The game is an RTS where the player's main objective will be to impose his religion on top of the others. You must collect resources well and choose your path: a chaotic one where you kill enemies or a peaceful one.

Samurai Shodown is a 1993 fighting game made by the SNK company. The initial game is becoming very popular and a total success. In the Project 1 subject of the Video Game Design and Development degree, we made a replica of the original game.

The game was made without an engine, directly from the C++ language using the SDL library, with the sprites obtained from the game of the time. However, we wanted to give it our own essence and we made a new character selection screen and a more extensive tutorial. In the project I extracted the sprites and programmed the different screens of the game (Main Menu, cinematics, tutorial, character selection, etc).

What if you suddenly met an enemy? Batrok’s Legacy is an augmented reality game that confronts you against the great Batrok, who will not hesitate for a second to attack you. With your projectiles you can defend yourself and be able to counteract his great fury.

This project has been created in 5 days within a group of 3 people using the Vuforia package for Unity. In this project I was in charge of the implementation of the libraries and the AI of the boss.

This project was carried out for the 3D Scenarios subject of the Video Game Design and Development degree. The main idea of the project was to create a new 3D setting for a game based on the world of The Witcher.

The following trailer shows various images of the player's journey through the map. Images of the group members also appear, providing information about the development and some design and art decisions of the project.

Soulters is the personal project I am currently working on. The name of Soulters comes from the composition of the words “soul” and “monster”. This name is the same as that given to the fantastic creatures of this virtual world.

1500 years ago there was a person who met a strange creature that gave him absolute power: being able to create new species on the planet. However, this power was used in a bad way and ended up being sealed by unknown heroes. Today, the curse is stronger again and our character will have to stand up to ensure the soulters and humans' protection. Along the way, the player will discover that the two species have a special link...

The fantastic world will be based in Terres de l’Ebre, an area in southern Catalonia where natural landscapes predominate. The project is currently in the script and reference collection phase.
